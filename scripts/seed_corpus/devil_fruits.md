# One Piece — Devil Fruits

## What are Devil Fruits?

Devil Fruits are mysterious fruits found throughout the world of One Piece. When eaten, they grant the user a unique superpower at the cost of their ability to swim — the sea rejects Devil Fruit users and they become permanently weakened in water. Only one fruit can be eaten per person; eating a second Devil Fruit kills the user.

Devil Fruits are said to be the Sea Devil's incarnations, and the powers they grant are called Devil Fruit abilities. When a Devil Fruit user dies, their power is reborn in another fruit somewhere in the world.

## Three Types of Devil Fruits

### Paramecia
The most common type. Paramecia fruits grant superhuman abilities that affect the user's body, produce substances, or manipulate the environment. Examples:
- **Gomu Gomu no Mi** (Gum-Gum Fruit) — Luffy: rubber body
- **Hana Hana no Mi** (Flower-Flower Fruit) — Robin: sprout body parts
- **Ope Ope no Mi** (Op-Op Fruit) — Law: creates a room where he controls everything
- **Bari Bari no Mi** (Barrier-Barrier Fruit) — Bartolomeo: creates unbreakable barriers
- **Mera Mera no Mi** (Flame-Flame Fruit) — Ace, then Sabo: control fire

### Zoan
Zoan fruits allow the user to transform into an animal or hybrid form. They are divided into:
- **Standard Zoan**: transform into regular animals (e.g., ox, wolf, giraffe)
- **Ancient Zoan**: transform into prehistoric creatures like dinosaurs — stronger than standard
- **Mythical Zoan**: the rarest type, transform into mythological creatures. Far stronger than Ancient Zoan and often come with additional special abilities

Notable Zoan users:
- **Hito Hito no Mi, Model: Nika** — Luffy: Mythical Zoan, Sun God transformation (Gear 5)
- **Hito Hito no Mi** — Chopper: Human-Human Fruit
- **Inu Inu no Mi, Model: Kyubi no Kitsune** — Catarina Devon: nine-tailed fox
- **Tori Tori no Mi, Model: Phoenix** — Marco: blue flame regeneration phoenix

### Logia
The rarest and most powerful type (aside from Mythical Zoan). Logia fruits allow users to transform into, create, and control a natural element. Logia users become intangible in their element form, making physical attacks pass through them unless the attacker uses Haki.
- **Magu Magu no Mi** (Magma-Magma Fruit) — Akainu: magma
- **Hie Hie no Mi** (Ice-Ice Fruit) — Aokiji: ice
- **Pika Pika no Mi** (Glint-Glint Fruit) — Kizaru: light
- **Yami Yami no Mi** (Dark-Dark Fruit) — Blackbeard: darkness
- **Goro Goro no Mi** (Rumble-Rumble Fruit) — Enel: lightning
- **Suna Suna no Mi** (Sand-Sand Fruit) — Crocodile: sand

## Devil Fruit Awakening

Some Devil Fruit users can awaken their powers, greatly increasing their strength. Awakening affects things beyond just the user's body:
- **Paramecia awakening**: the user can transform their surroundings into their element or substance
- **Zoan awakening**: dramatically increases physical abilities, speed, and recovery; the user's will can resist attempts to control them
- **Logia awakening**: not yet fully shown in canon

Notable awakenings:
- Luffy's Gear 5 is his Zoan awakening
- Doflamingo awakened his string fruit to transform his surroundings into strings
- Law and Kid both awakened their Paramecia fruits during the fight against Big Mom

## The Yami Yami no Mi — Special Properties

Blackbeard's Dark-Dark Fruit is unique among Logia fruits. Unlike other Logia users who become intangible, Blackbeard cannot nullify attacks — he takes full damage. However, his darkness can absorb anything, including other Devil Fruit powers. This is why Blackbeard was able to steal Whitebeard's Gura Gura no Mi (Tremor-Tremor Fruit) after Whitebeard's death, making Blackbeard the only known person to wield two Devil Fruit powers simultaneously.

## SMILE Artificial Devil Fruits

SMILE fruits are artificial Zoan Devil Fruits created by the scientist Caesar Clown using SAD, a substance produced only in Punk Hazard. They were manufactured for Kaido's army in Wano. Only 10% of SMILE fruits successfully grant powers — the remaining 90% cause the consumer to permanently lose the ability to express negative emotions, forced to laugh constantly.
