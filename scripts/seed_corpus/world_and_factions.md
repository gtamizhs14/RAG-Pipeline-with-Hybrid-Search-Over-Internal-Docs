# One Piece — The World and Major Factions

## The Grand Line

The Grand Line is a sea that runs across the middle of the world, dividing it into two halves — the North Blue, South Blue, East Blue, and West Blue on either side. It is known as the most dangerous and unpredictable sea in the world, with erratic weather, magnetic fields that make compasses useless, and sea creatures far larger and more dangerous than those in other seas.

The Grand Line is divided into two parts by the Red Line, a massive continent that circles the globe:
- **Paradise (first half)**: where most rookie pirates begin their journey
- **New World (second half)**: where the Emperors of the Sea rule and only the strongest pirates survive

At the center of the Grand Line lies **Raftel** (also called Laugh Tale), the island at the end of the Grand Line where the legendary treasure One Piece is said to be hidden. Only one crew has ever reached it — the Roger Pirates.

## The Four Emperors (Yonko)

The Four Emperors are the four most powerful pirates in the world, ruling the New World through sheer strength and influence. Post-Wano, the lineup has changed:

- **Shanks** (Red Hair Shanks): captain of the Red Hair Pirates, one of the few people who can stop wars with his presence alone
- **Marshall D. Teach** (Blackbeard): the only person known to have two Devil Fruit powers — the Dark-Dark Fruit and the Tremor-Tremor Fruit
- **Buggy the Clown**: elevated to Emperor status after the Wano incident due to his organization's influence
- **Monkey D. Luffy**: declared an Emperor after defeating Kaido in Wano

Former Emperors:
- **Kaido** of the Beasts: said to be the world's strongest creature, defeated by Luffy in Wano
- **Charlotte Linlin** (Big Mom): defeated by Law and Kid in Wano
- **Edward Newgate** (Whitebeard): the strongest man in the world until his death at Marineford

## The Marines

The Marines (also called the Navy) are the military sea force of the World Government, serving as the primary antagonistic force to pirates. They operate under the principle of "Absolute Justice."

Marine ranks from bottom to top: Chore Boy → Seaman → Petty Officer → Lieutenant → Commander → Captain → Commodore → Rear Admiral → Vice Admiral → Admiral → Fleet Admiral

The three Admirals are the strongest individual fighters in the Marines:
- **Akainu** (Sakazuki): Fleet Admiral, user of the Magma-Magma Fruit
- **Kizaru** (Borsalino): Admiral, user of the Glint-Glint Fruit (light)
- **Fujitora** (Issho): Admiral, user of the Gravity-Gravity Fruit

## The Seven Warlords of the Sea (Shichibukai)

The Shichibukai were seven powerful pirates who worked under the World Government in exchange for pardons and privileges. The system was abolished by a vote in the World Government after the Reverie. Notable former Warlords include: Dracule Mihawk (world's greatest swordsman), Boa Hancock (Empress of Amazon Lily), Jinbe (now a Straw Hat), Trafalgar Law, Donquixote Doflamingo, Crocodile, Bartholomew Kuma, and Gecko Moria.

## The Revolutionary Army

Led by Monkey D. Dragon — the world's most wanted man and Luffy's father — the Revolutionary Army actively fights against the World Government and the World Nobles. The Army's chief of staff is Sabo, Luffy's adoptive brother who ate the Flame-Flame Fruit after Ace's death.

## The World Government and the Celestial Dragons

The World Government is the ruling political entity of the One Piece world, governing 170 member nations. At its top are the Celestial Dragons (World Nobles) — descendants of the founders of the World Government who live in Mariejois and consider themselves gods above all other humans. They can summon an Admiral simply by writing a letter if anyone wrongs them.

## Poneglyphs and the Void Century

Poneglyphs are indestructible stone blocks inscribed with an ancient language that the World Government has made illegal to read or study. There are three types:
- **Historical Poneglyphs**: record history
- **Instructional Poneglyphs**: lead to other Poneglyphs
- **Road Poneglyphs**: four exist in total; their combined information points to the location of Laugh Tale

The Void Century is a 100-year period of history that the World Government has erased from all records. The Roger Pirates learned the truth of the Void Century by reaching Laugh Tale and reading all four Road Poneglyphs combined with Nico Robin's ability. The secret is tied to the "Will of D." and the Ancient Weapons.

## The Will of D.

Certain characters in One Piece carry the middle initial "D." — including Monkey D. Luffy, Monkey D. Dragon, Monkey D. Garp, Portgas D. Ace, Marshall D. Teach, Trafalgar D. Water Law, and Gol D. Roger. The meaning of the "D." is a mystery called the "Will of D." It is said that "D. will always cause a storm" and that those who carry it are natural enemies of the Celestial Dragons and the Gods.
