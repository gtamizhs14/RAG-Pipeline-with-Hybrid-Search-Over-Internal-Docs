# One Piece — Main Characters

## Monkey D. Luffy

Monkey D. Luffy is the protagonist of One Piece and the captain of the Straw Hat Pirates. His dream is to find the legendary treasure One Piece and become King of the Pirates. Luffy ate the Gomu Gomu no Mi (Gum-Gum Fruit), a Paramecia-type Devil Fruit that turned his body into rubber, making him immune to blunt impacts and electricity. He can stretch his limbs to great lengths and inflate his body.

Luffy later awakened the true nature of his fruit, which was revealed to be the Hito Hito no Mi, Model: Nika — the Mythical Zoan fruit of the Sun God Nika. In this awakened form called Gear 5, Luffy's body becomes white and his abilities become cartoonish and limitless, governed only by his imagination.

Luffy developed several power-ups called Gears:
- **Gear Second**: pumps blood faster, increasing speed and power dramatically
- **Gear Third**: blows air into his bones making his limbs giant
- **Gear Fourth**: combines Haki with rubber body into forms like Boundman, Tankman, and Snakeman
- **Gear Fifth**: awakened Zoan form, the most powerful transformation

Luffy is the son of Revolutionary Army leader Monkey D. Dragon and the grandson of Marine Vice Admiral Monkey D. Garp. His adoptive brothers are Portgas D. Ace (deceased) and Sabo.

## Roronoa Zoro

Roronoa Zoro is the first member to join the Straw Hat Pirates and serves as the crew's combatant and first mate. His dream is to become the world's greatest swordsman to honor the promise he made to his deceased childhood friend Kuina. Zoro uses a unique three-sword style called Santoryu, holding one sword in each hand and one in his mouth.

Zoro's swords include Wado Ichimonji (Kuina's sword), Sandai Kitetsu (a cursed blade), and Enma (a blade once wielded by the legendary samurai Kozuki Oden). Enma is notable for forcibly drawing out Haki from its wielder.

Zoro is one of the two members of the crew capable of using Conqueror's Haki, the other being Luffy. After training with Dracule Mihawk — the world's greatest swordsman — during the two-year timeskip, Zoro's power increased dramatically. His most powerful techniques include Asura, where he creates a nine-sword illusion, and his post-timeskip techniques that can cut through steel and even fire.

## Nami

Nami is the navigator of the Straw Hat Pirates. Her dream is to draw a complete map of the entire world. She grew up in Cocoyasi Village under the control of the Arlong Pirates. She joined the Straw Hats after Luffy defeated Arlong and freed her village.

Nami uses a weapon called the Clima-Tact, developed by Usopp, which can manipulate weather on a small scale. After the timeskip, it was upgraded to the Sorcery Clima-Tact, and later enhanced further with Zeus, a thundercloud homie from Big Mom. Nami can create thunderstorms, tornadoes, and mirages in battle.

## Sanji

Vinsmoke Sanji is the cook of the Straw Hat Pirates. His dream is to find the All Blue, a legendary sea where all the world's fish are found. Sanji fights exclusively with his legs, never using his hands in combat to protect them for cooking.

Sanji is the third son of the Vinsmoke Family, rulers of the Germa Kingdom. He was genetically modified before birth to have superhuman abilities, though his modifications were thought to be suppressed. After the Whole Cake Island arc, his modifications activated, giving him exoskeleton-like durability.

Sanji also awakened the ability to use Ifrit Jambe, a combination of his Raid Suit enhancements and his Diable Jambe fire technique, generating blue flames far hotter than his red flames.

## Tony Tony Chopper

Tony Tony Chopper is the doctor of the Straw Hat Pirates. He ate the Hito Hito no Mi (Human-Human Fruit), a Zoan-type Devil Fruit that gave him human intelligence and the ability to transform. He uses a drug called Rumble Ball to access additional transformations beyond the standard three Zoan forms. His dream is to become a doctor capable of curing any disease.

## Nico Robin

Nico Robin is the archaeologist of the Straw Hat Pirates. She ate the Hana Hana no Mi (Flower-Flower Fruit), which allows her to sprout copies of her body parts — arms, legs, eyes — on any surface. Her dream is to find the Rio Poneglyph, which tells the true history of the world known as the Void Century. She is the only person alive who can read the ancient language on Poneglyphs.

## Usopp

Usopp is the sniper of the Straw Hat Pirates. His dream is to become a brave warrior of the sea like his father, Yasopp, a member of Shanks' Red Hair Pirates. Usopp is a master liar and inventor who creates special ammunition and weapons. After the timeskip, he trained his Observation Haki to an advanced level called Kenbunshoku Haki, allowing him to see slightly into the future.

## Franky

Franky is the shipwright of the Straw Hat Pirates. He built the crew's ship, the Thousand Sunny. Franky is a cyborg who modified his own body using cola as fuel. His dream is to build a ship that sails around the entire world. His body contains weapons including cannons, drills, and laser beams added after the timeskip.

## Brook

Brook is the musician of the Straw Hat Pirates. He ate the Yomi Yomi no Mi (Revive-Revive Fruit), which allowed his soul to return to his body after death, making him a living skeleton. His dream is to reunite with his old friend Laboon, a whale at the entrance to the Grand Line. Brook can use his Devil Fruit powers to separate his soul from his body and use Soul Solid, a technique that freezes anything his sword touches.

## Jinbe

Jinbe is the helmsman of the Straw Hat Pirates and a former Warlord of the Sea. He is a Fish-Man who specializes in Fish-Man Karate, which manipulates water and body fluids. He was formerly a member of the Sun Pirates and a close friend of the late Portgas D. Ace. His dream is to see humans and Fish-Men coexist peacefully.
